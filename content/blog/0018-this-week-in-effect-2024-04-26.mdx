### Content Update

On Monday we posted one of the most anticipated talks from the Effect Days conference, "Why Effect is more important than ZIO" by [John De Goes](https://twitter.com/jdegoes). John is the author of [ZIO](https://zio.dev) & founder of [Ziverge](https://www.ziverge.com) and [Golem Cloud](https://www.golem.cloud), and also, Grandfather of Effect (cit. [Sebatian Lorenz](https://twitter.com/thefubhy)).

In this talk, John discusses the history and design decisions of ZIO, the birth and evolution of Effect, and the reasons why he believes Effect is here to stay and will have a bigger impact on commercial functional programming than ZIO.

<YouTube url="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Ei6VTwhI8QQ?si=II9_bM-3b4iyiyEh" />
