## Episode Notes

Louis Vichy, co-founder of [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/), joins Johannes Schickling and Michael Arnaldi to talk about OpenRouter’s TypeScript stack, internal tooling powered by Effect, and the engineering challenges of scaling an AI platform processing trillions of tokens weekly.

Effect is an ecosystem of tools for building robust, production-grade applications in TypeScript.
